Profile:

  • Runmin Cong (IEEE/CCF/CSIG Senior Member, ACM/APSIPA IVM Member) is currently a Professor with the School of Control Science and Engineering, Shandong University (SDU), Jinan, China. He received the Ph.D. degree in information and communication engineering from Tianjin University, Tianjin, China, in June 2019. Before joining SDU, he was an Associate Professor with the Institute of Information Science, Beijing Jiaotong University, Beijing, China. He was a visiting student/staff at Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore, and City University of Hong Kong (CityU), Hong Kong SAR. His research interests include computer vision, machine learning, multimedia processing and understanding, saliency computation, remote sensing image interpretation and analysis, and visual content enhancement in an open environment, etc. He has published more than 70 papers in prestigious international journals and conferences, including 55 IEEE/ACM Trans/CCF-A papers, 2 ESI hot papers (Top 0.1%), 11 ESI highly cited papers (Top 1%), 19 papers were selected as IEEE Popular Documents, and 16 China patents have been authorized. He serves/served as an Associate Editor/Editorial Board Member of the Neurocomputing, the IEEE Journal of Oceanic Engineering, the Signal, Image and Video Processing, International Journal of Interactive Multimedia and Artificial Intelligence, the Youth Editorial Board Member of the CAAI Transactions on Intelligence Technology, the Guest Editor of the IEEE Journal of Oceanic Engineering, Drones, the Signal Processing: Image Communication, and the Multimedia Tools and Applications, and the AC/SPC/PC of NeurIPS, CVPR, ICML, ICCV, ECCV, SIGGRAPH, ACM MM, AAAI, IJCAI, ICME, ICPR, PRCV, ICMR, etc. Dr. Cong was a recipient of the Young Elite Scientist Sponsorship Program by the China Association for Science and Technology, the Beijing Nova Program, the The World’s Top 2% Scientists (2020, 2021), the Youth Talent Development Program of Beijing Jiaotong University, IEEE ICME Best Student Paper Award Runner-Up, First Prize for Scientific and Technological Progress Award of Tianjin Municipality, ACM SIGWEB China Rising Star Award, IEEE CVPR 2021 Outstanding Reviewer, 2021 IEEE Journal of Oceanic Engineering Outstanding Reviewer, Excellent Doctorial Dissertation Award from CSIG and BSIG, Excellent Scientific Paper Award for Beijing Youth.

Research Interests:

  • Attention Perception and Saliency Computation:
    Exploring the salient object detection and segmentation solutions for RGB images, RGB-D images, related image groups, video sequences, light field images, 360° panoramic images, etc.
  • Visual Content Enhancement in An Open Environment:
    Exploring various content enhancement issues in different environments, including the quality assessment/enhancement/restoration/super-resolution/reconstruction of low-light/haze/underwater/blurred/reflection/low-resolution/LDR image and video, etc.
  • Multimedia Big Data Understanding and Application:
    Exploring information processing and content understanding solutions for cross-media and cross-modal data, including referring image comprehension and segmentation, cross-media information retrieval, and visual question answering, etc.
  • Interpretation and Analysis of Remote Sensing Image:
    Exploring solutions for the classification, segmentation, retrieval, reconstruction, and detection of optical remote sensing images, hyperspectral images, radar images, UAV images, etc.
  • Advanced Machine Learning Theory and Methodology:
    Exploring machine learning related theories and practices, such as zero-shot/few-shot learning, self-supervised/weakly-supervised learning, continual learning, contrastive learning, meta learning, etc.
