Profile:

  • My name is Runmin Cong, an Associate Professor at Center of Digital Media Information Processing (MePro), Institute of Information Science, Beijing Jiaotong University, China. I received the Ph.D. degree in information and communication engineering from Tianjin University, Tianjin, China, in June 2019. I have published more than 30 papers in prestigious international journals and conferences, including 15 IEEE Trans/CCF-A papers (5 TIP, 3 TCyb, 3 TMM, 1 TCSVT, 1 TGRS, 1CVPR, 1 AAAI), and 9 China patents have been authorized. I am a recipient of the Best Student Paper Award Runner-Up at IEEE ICME in 2018, First Prize for Scientific and Technological Progress Award of Tianjin Municipality, Excellent Doctorial Dissertation Award from CSIG and BSIG, Excellent Scientific Paper Award for Beijing Youth, National Scholarship, and Special Award of Innovation Scholarship in Tianjin Municipality. I am also lucky to have opportunities to collaborate with Weisi Lin (IEEE/IET Fellow and Professor at NTU, Singapore), Sam Kwong (IEEE Fellow and Chair Professor at CityU, Hong Kong SAR), Fatih Porikli (IEEE Fellow and Professor at ANU, Australia), Huazhu Fu (Senior Research Scientist at IIAI, Abu Dhabi), Xiaochun Cao (IET Fellow and Professor at IIE CAS, China), Ming-Ming Cheng (Professor at NKU, China), Junhui Hou (Assistant Professor at CityU, Hong Kong SAR), Chongyi Li (Research Fellow at NTU, Singapore), Qianqian Xu (Associate Professor at ICT CAS, China), Changqing Zhang (Associate Professor at TJU, China), and Wenguan Wang (Research Fellow at ETH, Switzerland).
