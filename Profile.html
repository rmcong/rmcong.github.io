Profile:

  • My name is Runmin Cong, an Associate Professor at Institute of Information Science, Beijing Jiaotong University, China. I received the Ph.D. degree in information and communication engineering from Tianjin University, Tianjin, China, in June 2019. My current research interests include computer vision and intelligent video system, saliency detection and segmentation, remote sensing image processing, and deep learning. I have published more than 20 SCI/EI papers in prestigious international journals and conferences, including 11 IEEE Trans papers (4 TIP, 2 TCyb, 3 TMM, 1 TCSVT, 1 TGRS), and 4 China patents have been authorized. I won the Best Student Paper Award Runner-Up at IEEE ICME in 2018, First Prize for Scientific and Technological Progress Award of Tianjin Municipality, Excellent Doctoral Degree Dissertation Award from BSIG, National Scholarship, and Special Award of Innovation Scholarship in Tianjin Municipality.

    From Dec. 2016 to Feb. 2017, I worked with Prof. Weisi Lin (IEEE/IET Fellow) as a visiting student at School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore. From May 2018 to May 2019, I worked with Chair Prof. Sam Kwong (IEEE Fellow) and Prof. Junhui Hou as a Research Associate at Department of Computer Science, City University of Hong Kong (CityU), Hong Kong. In addition, I am also lucky to have opportunities to collaborate with Fatih Porikli (IEEE Fellow and Professor at ANU, Australia), Huazhu Fu (Senior Research Scientist at IIAI, Abu Dhabi), Xiaochun Cao (IET Fellow and Professor at IIE CAS, China), Ming-Ming Cheng (Professor at NKU, China), Chongyi Li (Postdoctoral Fellow at CityU, Hong Kong), Changqing Zhang (Assistant Professor at TJU, China), and Wenguan Wang (Senior Research Scientist at IIAI, Abu Dhabi).
