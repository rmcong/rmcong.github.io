Profile:

  • My name is Runmin Cong, a final year Ph.D candidate at School of Electrical and Information Engineering, Tianjin University, P.R. China, supervised by Prof. Qingming Huang (IEEE Fellow) and Prof. Jianjun Lei. My current research interests include computer vision and intelligent video system, saliency detection and segmentation, remote sensing image processing, and deep learning. I won the Best Student Paper Award Runner-Up at IEEE ICME in 2018, First Prize for Scientific and Technological Progress Award of Tianjin Municipality, National Scholarship, Excellent Doctoral Degree Dissertation Award from BSIG, and Special Award of Innovation Scholarship in Tianjin Municipality.

    From Dec. 2016 to Feb. 2017, I worked with Prof. Weisi Lin (IEEE/IET Fellow) as a visiting student at School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore. Since May 2018, I have been working with Chair Prof. Sam Kwong (IEEE Fellow) and Prof. Junhui Hou as a Research Associate at Department of Computer Science, City University of Hong Kong (CityU), Hong Kong. In addition, I am also lucky to have opportunities to collaborate with Fatih Porikli (IEEE Fellow and Professor at ANU, Australia), Huazhu Fu (Senior Research Scientist at IIAI, Abu Dhabi), Xiaochun Cao (Professor at IIE CAS, China), Ming-Ming Cheng (Professor at NKU, China), Chongyi Li (Postdoctoral Fellow at CityU, Hong Kong), Changqing Zhang (Assistant Professor at TJU, China), and Wenguan Wang (Senior Research Scientist at IIAI, Abu Dhabi).
