INTRODUCTION

The Multimedia and Vision Processing Laboratory was established in July 2019. It is affiliated to the Center of Digital Media Information Processing (MePro) at Beijing Jiaotong University, and the director is Dr. Runmin Cong. There are more than 20 graduate students and undergraduate interns, mainly engaged in multimedia understanding, vision processing and other related research fields.
